Redesigned Netflix NavBar Micro Interaction

Triggers

On-Hover, Off-Hover.

Rules

On-Hover:

- First create a dark gray, ellipse behind the icon.

- Next expand the ellipse to the right.

- Next fade in text respective of the icon's destination.

- Finally draw a white underline starting from left, expanding to the right.

Off-Hover:

Rewind the effects of On-Hover.

Feedback

Visual:

On-Hover, the darkened font indicates/validates pointer focus.

Loops & Modes

None.
